The Woman
Behind the Work

About
"She doesn't just advise on art — she is the art. Every room she enters, every brand she touches, every screen she graces."

Stacy Kessler is a New York City–based multi-hyphenate operating at the intersection of art, entertainment, and influence. Founder of Stacy Kessler Art Advisory and Stacy Kessler Accessories — a sustainable fashion brand holding a US design and utility patent — she brings an MBA from Fordham University, a Master's in Communications from Wright State, and a BA from Tel Aviv University to every engagement.

Fluent in English, Spanish, Hebrew, and French, she operates across seven international markets. A 2023 Luce Foundation Community Leadership Award recipient, Stacy is as committed to her community as she is to her craft.
